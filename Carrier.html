Message
Mr. Fateh Ullah Khan
Minister for Planning & Development and Information
Government of Gilgit-Baltistan
It gives me a great pleasure to express that I address to the people who are not only the air time consumers but are more concerned about the quality service and expecting the added from the service providers. There were the days when even an important news usually took a long time to spread in the region where we live, but I am very thankful to Special Communications Organization (SCO) for minimizing distances in our lives. It is also important to note what has not changed at communication; it is SCO’s commitment in providing exceptional service. The year 2020 marked major turning point for our society. Along with the heightening of uncertainties brought about by COVID-19, people’s life style and work style, as well as medical, educational, and other social systems as a whole underwent major changes. Through these changes, we have experienced the great power and value of digital technologies. These digitally stimulated social changes are most likely to continue from now onward. I would like to see Ahmed Communications (Pvt) Ltd. (ACPL) stand at the center and be the driver of such dynamic changes. Finally, I am very optimistic that Ahmed Communications (Pvt.) Ltd. (ACPL) will prove itself to be a true enterprise enthusiast and play the role model to the youth of Gilgit-Baltistan so that together we would be able to continue progress.
