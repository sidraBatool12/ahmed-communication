About Ahmed Communication

Ahmed Communications (Pvt.) Ltd. (ACPL) is a leading telecommunication Franchise of Gilgit-Baltistan represents Special Communications Organization (SCO) providing innovative and state of the art communication services in Gilgit-Baltistan. We are a brand synonymous to superior customer care and reliability in services. Being part of the Special Communications Organization we aim to empower our customers with the choice of dynamic lifestyle in the GB region. We work to offer value for money with a diversified product portfolio; PSTN, VDSL, DSL, GSM, DXX, LL and Tripple Play TV App®. We are offering products ranging from advance data tools to high speed Internet. Our Tripple Play Service (TPS®) is a tremendous addition in our offering Ahmed Communication (Pvt.) Ltd. (ACPL) is one of the largest and oldest telecom service providers franchise that have been operating in almost all the districts of Gilgit-Baltistan in past two decades in association with SCOM (SCO). Being the first franchise company in GB, Ahmed Communication (Pvt.) Ltd. was selected by “SCO” on priority due to our high performance for customers & innovative approach towards our products and services. We lead the race, we defined the market, we motivated high spirits in the youth of our hands. Eventually a small enterprise reshaped its existence and became a private limited company.

Guided by our values rooted in us, we strive to shape a better future continuing the legacy of our founder Late Mr. Arif Mannan who designed ACPL 15 years ago. Our services are available to individuals, corporate entities and governments as well. We have diversified our business portfolio and today we are a major lead player in the market. With further aim to offer ultimate experience with highest customer care at the most affordable price with dedication to offer an affordable price. In today’s competitive business environment, ACPL has emerged as one of the ultimate personalized voice and data service provider in the region, for a modern commercial society. Today we are a progressive, efficient, and excellent focused institution. Our long term value creation model is based on the balanced combination of financial profitability, environmental protection and social responsibility. We offer the best service on the basis of customer choice and our standing, distinctively with our parent organization “The SCO” whose purpose is to unlock the power of communication to enhance quality of life for everyone, today and for generations to come.
